Rob's Chicken Enchilada Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Shredded Chicken (1/2 rotisserie recommended)
  • Flour Tortillas
  • 1 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 1/4 Cup of Chopped Shallot
  • 2 Tablespoons of Olive Oil (or butter)
  • 1/2 Cup of Monterey Jack Cheese (or Mexican blend)
  • 1 Small Can of Chopped Green Chiles (7oz)
  • 1 Green Onion (for garnish)
  • Handful of Chopped Italian Parsley (for garnish)
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in saute pan on medium-low.
  2. Pour in shallots and chopped green chiles and saute for a few minutes until fragrant.
  3. Turn off heat and mix in shredded chicken.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Spoon mixture into flour tortillas and roll up each one, placing each side-by-side in baking dish.
  6. After all rolled enchiladas are placed in baking pan, pour in heavy cream.
  7. Top with cheese and bake in pre-heated oven at 350 degrees for 20 minutes or until cheese melts and lightly browns.
  8. Remove from oven, add garnish and serve.
