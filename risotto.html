Rob's Risotto Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 Cups of Arborio Rice
  • 1/2 Cup Grated Parmigiano-Reggiano Cheese
  • 6 Cups of Chicken Broth
  • 1/4 Cup of Finely Chopped Shallot
  • 2 Tablespoons of Olive Oil (or butter)
  • 2 Cloves of Finely Chopped Garlic
  • 1/2 Cup of White Wine
  • Handful of Chopped Italian Parsley (for garnish)
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in saute pan on medium-low.
  2. At the same time, in a different pot, bring chicken broth to a near boil.
  3. Pour in shallots and garlic into saute pan and saute for a few minutes until fragrant.
  4. Mix in dry Arborio Rice and 'toast' rice for about 3 minutes, stirring frequently.
  5. Mix in white wine and allow it to reduce, about 3-5 minutes until it halves in volume.
  6. Turn heat up to medium-high and pour in 1 cup of chicken broth and stir continuously until broth evaporates.
  7. Once broth evaporate, pour in more chicken broth just enough to cover the rice, stir continuously until it evaporates.
  8. Repeat the above step until rice grain turns opaque, about 20 minutes or so and validate by tasting a rice grain - the texture should be cooked but have a light firmness to it.
  9. Turn off heat and mix in Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese.
  10. Garnish with more cheese and chopped Italian Parsley.

Finished and plated dish.
